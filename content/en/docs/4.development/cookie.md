## What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites save on your computer or mobile device when you visit the site. They help the website remember your actions and preferences, like items in your shopping cart or your login details.

## Annoying Cookies

While cookies can be useful, they can also be annoying. They take up space on your device, they can slow down your computer, and they can sometimes even be used by malicious websites to track you and your activities.

## How to Avoid Annoying Cookies 

1. **Clear your browsers cookies regularly.** You can usually do this by going to your browsers settings or preferences, then selecting the option to clear cookies.

2. **Use a different browser.** If youre not happy with the cookies on a particular website, you can try using a different browser. Most modern browsers automatically block cookies from sites you havent visited recently.

3. **Use a browser that doesnt automatically accept cookies.** Some browsers allow you to control whether they accept cookies from websites.

4. **Use a VPN.** _RISKY_ A Virtual Private Network (VPN) can help you browse the internet anonymously, which can make you less likely to be tracked by websites that use cookies to track your activities. Your data goes through unknown servers, so use with caution.
