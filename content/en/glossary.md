::GlossaryGroup
---
title: The space
terms:
  - word: Lähde
    translation: source
    description: The community's repository. Everything flows from here.
    featured: true
  - word: Haara
    translation: branch
    description: A subset of the community working on something specific.
  - word: Pohja
    translation: floor
    description: The bottom layer. The full history of what the community has held.
---
::

::GlossaryGroup
---
title: The things being carried
terms:
  - word: Pisara
    translation: drop
    description: A single unit of community knowledge. A purpose, a question, something that keeps surfacing. Small enough to carry, specific enough to matter.
    featured: true
  - word: Kantaja
    translation: carrier
    description: The person holding a pisara.
  - word: Kantama
    translation: carried
    description: The state of a pisara that someone is holding.
  - word: Haihtumassa
    translation: evaporating
    description: A pisara nobody picked up. Not lost, becoming water vapor.
  - word: Vajonnut
    translation: sunk
    description: A pisara that went to the bottom.
---
::

phases:

    description: Where it begins.
  - word: Virta
    translation: current
    description: Things moving.
  - word: Suvanto
    translation: still water
    description: Integration. Things moving underground.
  - word: Pyörre
    translation: whirlpool
    description: Circling something before knowing which way to go.
  - word: Koski
    translation: rapids
    description: Fast, loud, difficult.
  - word: Vesiraja
    translation: waterline
    description: Where above and below become visible at once.
  - word: Meri
    translation: the sea
    description: Completion. The water that reached the end of this cycle.
