The water is always in some state.

- Sometimes it is calm and you can see straight to the bottom.
- Sometimes it is rising and everything feels possible.
- Sometimes something difficult is moving through

and the only right thing is to be present without adding to the turbulence.

You can signal how the water feels to you.

If enough people sense the same thing, it surfaces.
