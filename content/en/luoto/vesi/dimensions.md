## DimensionsMap

An animated SVG cross-section of the water world. Four horizontal layers run the full
width, **Pilvi**, **Pinta**, **Pohjavesi**, **Pohja**, crossed by six phase zones
along the x-axis. Click any zone badge to highlight it.

::DimensionsMap
::

## Zones

| Zone | Finnish | Description |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Lähde | Spring | Water rises from the earth through bedrock |
| Virta | Stream | Steady current flowing downstream |
| Suvanto & Pyörre | Pool & Eddy | Still water beside a swirling vortex |
| Koski | Rapids | Turbulent water crashing over exposed rock |
| Vesiraja | Shoreline | The boundary where land meets sea |
| Meri | Sea | Open water, the journey's end |

## Layers

| Layer | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Pilvi** | Sky, clouds drift at varying speeds |
| **Pinta** | Surface, animated wave, spring glow at Lähde, spray at Koski |
| **Pohjavesi** | Underground, slow particle flow through bedrock |
| **Pohja** | Deepest layer, still and dark |
