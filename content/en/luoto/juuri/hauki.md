Hauki swallows what you type. He sinks. He surfaces with a verdict. He has done this since before the first deadline.

## What is this

A peer-reviewed, clinically meaningless tool for the scientific analysis of keyboard smashing. Submit your smash, receive your diagnosis.

## How to smash

Type anything into the input field. Do not think. Do not plan. Let the keyboard speak.

## Classifications

| Type | Trigger |
|---|---|
| The Timid Tap | Less than 4 characters |
| The Broken Key Incident | Same key 6+ times in a row |
| The Punctuation Crisis | 40%+ special characters |
| THE SCREAMER | Predominantly uppercase |
| Absolute Bedlam | High chaos, high variety |
| Accidental Haiku | Surprisingly poetic distribution |
| The Deadline Special | Long, desperate, capitalized |
| The Gentle Wrist Roll | Low chaos, low energy |
| Standard Emotional Release | Everything else |

## Metrics

- **Chaos Score**, unpredictability of character distribution
- **Desperation**, length and intensity combined
- **Accidental Poetry**, unique characters and spacing rhythm
- **Screaming Energy**, uppercase to lowercase ratio

## Certification

Every smash receives an official certification. These are meaningless but accurate.

## Verdict

A single sentence from Hauki. Do not argue with it. He was here before you and will remain after.
