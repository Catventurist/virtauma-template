> **"There is an unfinished field."**

Pellervoinen is the spirit of the unplowed field. Not emptiness. Potential that has not yet been touched by the right hands.

You are those hands now.

## Tuonetar

The portti opens for everyone. She has not yet taken her place at the threshold.
`useAuth` exists, the cookie shape is decided, the roles are named Muukalainen,
Tietäjä, Väinämöinen, but the door is not yet guarded. This is the first thing
the field needs.

## Vellamo

Her waters have been named. The `lake` palette exists, Finlandica is loaded,
Päivätär and Kuutar wait in their respective skies. But she has not been shaped
to the full surface of the application yet. Present but not yet at home everywhere
she should be.

## Etsi

She sees the collection and returns results. But she does not yet know what matters
more than what. No ranking, no weights, no memory of what people were looking for
when they arrived. The two experiences, Vipunen for docs and Luode for guides, are
separate but not yet fully distinct in how they serve.

## Välimuisti

He watches the door but Rahko has not yet given him a schedule. Invalidation is
still manual. The water model is clear, Jää, Vuorovesi, Joki, Tekojärvi, Sade,
but it has not been fully laid into the ground. Some of the field is still frozen.

## Ikiturso

He sleeps in the deep for now. The Turso layer is a future thing, not a missing
thing. When the time comes he will be woken properly, with boundaries and care.
Not yet.

The field is real. The soil is good. What it needs now is time and the right hands
returning to it, season by season, the way fields have always been tended.

**Come back when you are ready. The field waits.**
