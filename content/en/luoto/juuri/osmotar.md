Osmotar did not rush the brew. She gathered, combined, waited. The ale was ready when it was ready, not before.

Development in Virtauma walks the same path.

## The Depths

Items do not move on a schedule. They rise when the conditions are right.

| Depth | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Pinta | Surfaced, ready or released |
| Virta | In the current, nearly ready |
| Syvyys | Fermenting, active but not done |
| Pohja | Raw ingredients, not yet combined |
| Kaiku | The empty cup, remembered |

## The Kinds

| Kind | What it is |
|---|---|
| Feature | Something new being brewed |
| Fix | Something that went wrong being corrected |
| Ritual | A recurring practice being tended |
| Lore | Documentation or mythology being written |

## Notes

Nothing here is a promise. Osmotar does not give guarantees. She gives direction.
