> **"Know the origin, and you know everything."**

The old wizards did not heal sickness by knowing its symptoms. They healed it by
knowing its **syntytieto**. Where it came from, when it first stepped into the world,
whose hands set it in motion.

**When you know where something came from, you know what to do with it.**

Iron was born from a meteorite, a stone fallen from the sky. That is why iron held a
power that other metals did not. Fire was born from the sky's strike, from Ukko's
hammer. That is why fire feared water but not man. Every thing in the world carried
its origin with it, invisible but real, like a growth ring inside a tree.

::callout{icon="i-lucide-flame" color="warning"}
A wizard who knew the charm but not its origin was not a wizard. He was just a
voice. Words without roots do not catch on anything.
::

The same wisdom holds today. Before you fix a bug, find out where it was born. Before
you build something, understand why it needs to exist. Syntytieto is not history for
history's sake. It is a practical tool.

**Old knowledge does not age. It simply waits for someone to ask the right question.**
