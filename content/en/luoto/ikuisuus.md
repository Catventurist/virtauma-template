Ikuisuus, Finnish for *eternity*, is a content component that treats time as a river rather than a line.

Items emerge from the depths, drift upward through your field of vision, and return to the water transformed. Each cycle ages them, language shifts, clarity fades, until they dissolve back into silence.

Hover to slow the current. Click to anchor an item against the flow. Let go and watch it rejoin the river, a little older than before.

Nothing is lost. Everything is forgotten eventually.

## Example

::ikuisuus
---
speed: 0.6
cycles: 4
height: 400
items:
  - id: 1
    content: The river remembers every stone it has touched
  - id: 2
    content: Time is not a line but a current returning to itself
  - id: 3
    content: What is forgotten was never truly lost
  - id: 4
    content: The depth holds what the surface cannot
  - id: 5
    content: Every wave has crossed this shore before
---
::
