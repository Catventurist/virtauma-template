## Ajatar

Ajatar lives in the wilderness, on the slopes of hills. She cannot be caught, cannot be tamed, cannot be stopped. 

She moves by her own path, without promises, without schedules. But she always returns. You know she has been there by what she leaves behind.

Virtauma's development walks the same path. Not linear, not scheduled. Cyclical, deepening, its own.

### First Cycle - Foundation

Ajatar walks through the forest first. She feels the ground.

- Vipunen, knowledge and docs
- Ikiturso, users and community data
- Tuonetar, authentication and roles
- Tiera, navigation
- Vellamo, appearance
- Kielet, multilingual

### Second Cycle - Community

She returns. Deeper this time.

- Comments and reactions
- User profiles
- Notifications
- Following

### Third Cycle - Life

Ajatar no longer just visits. She stays.

- Real-time presence
- Collaborative editing
- Moderation
- _Analytics_

### Fourth Cycle and Beyond

Ajatar does not end. Every cycle reveals a new path. The deeper you go, the more forest there is.

> **Do not ask when she returns. Ask what she brings with her.**
