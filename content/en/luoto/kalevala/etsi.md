Väinämöinen did not find lost knowledge by guessing. He descended, first through Manala, then into Vipunen's mouth.

Search is the same journey. You ask, and the depths answer.

In Virtauma there are two descents. Both seek, but from different waters.

### Vipunen

Docs search lives in its own space. It searches Vipunen's words, the source of learning, the unchanging depth.

### Luode

Luode is its own world, guides, paths, directions. Its search is separate, its own layout, its own rhythm.

### Two Descents

Etsi does not unite these two. Vipunen knows the documents, Luode knows the paths. Different questions need different descents.

> **The right question always finds its answer.**
