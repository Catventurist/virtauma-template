The Resource Pool lets your community share what it has, tools, skills, licenses, knowledge, money, and find what it needs. Everything is transparent, tracked and governed by trust.

## Getting started

Add the dashboard to any page by dropping in the `PoolDashboard` component with the current member's role and id.

Members with no account see a blurred view of what exists but cannot request anything.

## Offering a resource

Any member can offer a resource. Click **Offer Resource**, describe what you are sharing, set the type and choose who can access it, open to all, members only, verified or fully trusted members.

## Requesting a resource

Click **Request** on any available resource. The owner will approve or decline. You will see the status update in real time. You cannot request the same resource twice while a request is pending.

## Trust levels

The pool uses four trust levels to control access:

- **Open**, anyone including guests can see and request
- **Member**, any logged in community member
- **Verified**, members who have been vouched for by others
- **Trusted**, long standing members with a strong history

## Wants

If you need something the pool does not have, post a want. The community can see it and offer a matching resource. Wants are automatically closed if a matching resource already exists.

## For stewards

Stewards see the full analytics dashboard, utilization rates, idle resources, top contributors and pending requests. They can approve, decline, flag and retire resources. Use this to keep the pool healthy and active.
