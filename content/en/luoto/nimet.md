Nothing in Virtauma is named by accident. The names come from Finnish mythology, the Kalevala, the old beliefs that ran beneath the surface of everyday life long before anyone wrote them down.

Each one was chosen because the myth already contained the concept. The naming was recognition, not invention.

## On the untranslatable

Some names resist translation. Syntytieto has no English equivalent. Hämy is not quite twilight. Revontulet is not quite aurora. This is intentional. A platform built on Finnish tradition should carry words that only exist in Finnish.

If you encounter a name you do not recognise, follow it. The myth will tell you what the feature does.
