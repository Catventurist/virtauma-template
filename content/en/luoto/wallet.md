The wallet displays your token holdings across five water states, from volatile steam to crystallized rime. Each token represents a different relationship to value, time, and commitment.

::

## Tokens

| Token | State | Role |
|-------|-------|------|
| HÖYRY | Steam | Volatile reputation, burns fast |
| SULA | Liquid | Base currency, flows freely |
| VIRTA | Current | Earned through contribution |
| JÄÄ | Ice | Frozen staked value |
| HUURRE | Rime | Crystallized long-term store |

## Phase Bar

The temperature bar at the top of the wallet shows your portfolio's weighted average temperature. A cold wallet is heavily staked most value locked in JÄÄ or HUURRE. A hot wallet holds mostly HÖYRY and SULA, liquid and ready to move.

## Freeze & Melt

You can move value between liquid and frozen states at any time.

**Freeze** converts SULA → JÄÄ. Use this to stake, commit to a community, or slow your spending.

**Melt** converts JÄÄ → SULA. Thaws your stake back into liquid currency. Some actions may impose a delay before melting is possible.

## Earning Tokens

- **HÖYRY** generated by high-frequency activity, decays quickly
- **SULA** received in transfers, payments, or community distributions
- **VIRTA** rewarded for sustained contribution over time
- **JÄÄ** only acquired by freezing SULA
- **HUURRE** forms slowly from long-term JÄÄ holdings
