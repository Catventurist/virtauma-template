## Steps

### Tervetuloa

Choose your language. The platform supports Finnish and English throughout.

### Identity

Pick a name and generate a wallet address. Your address is auto-generated on load,
hit **Generate** to create a new one. The name must be between 2 and 32 characters.

### First SULA

Every new account receives **100 SULA** on creation. SULA is the base liquid currency
of the platform- it flows freely between members and communities.

### Freeze

Optionally lock some SULA into JÄÄ. Frozen tokens signal commitment and unlock
staking benefits. Use the slider to choose how much to freeze. You can melt JÄÄ
back into SULA at any time from your wallet.

| State | Token | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| Liquid | SULA | Spendable, transferable |
| Frozen | JÄÄ | Staked, earns over time |

### Community

Join one of the available communities for 10 SULA. Communities are the primary
unit of organization on the platform- they have shared pools, governance, and
contribution tracking. You can join more communities later.

### Valmis

Your wallet is ready. You will enter the platform with your SULA, JÄÄ,
and community membership in place.

## Validation

Each step validates before proceeding:

- **Name**- 2 to 32 characters
- **Address**- auto-generated, minimum 10 characters
- **Freeze**- 0 to 100 SULA
- **Community**- must select one or skip
