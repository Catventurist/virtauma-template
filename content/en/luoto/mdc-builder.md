title: MDC Builder Guide
description: A quick guide to get you started
---

The MDC Builder lets you visually compose Nuxt Content MDC components and get the markup generated for you automatically.

## Getting started

Pick any component from the left sidebar — you can search by name or filter by category. Click it to drop it straight onto the canvas.

## Editing props

Click any node on the canvas to select it. The right panel will show all available props for that component. Changes reflect instantly in the output below.

## Reordering

Grab the grip handle on the left of any node and drag it up or down to reorder. The other nodes will slide out of the way.

## The output

The bottom panel shows your generated MDC markup in real time. Switch to JSON if you need a structured format instead. Hit copy or download when you're done.

## Shortcuts

You can undo and redo with **Cmd+Z** and **Cmd+Shift+Z**. Nodes can be duplicated with the copy button or removed with the x button.
