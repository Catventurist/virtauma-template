## Aino

Aino walked into the water and did not come back. Not lost, remembered. She became part of the sea, preserved in it, unchanging. 

Väinämöinen saw her again as a fish, reached out, and she slipped away. She was there. She was real. But she could not be called back.

Every release is Aino. It wades into the water and becomes part of the record. 

You can read it, remember it, trace its ripples, but you cannot undo it. It is there, preserved, a fish that slipped away.

### The Record

Aino is powered by Markdown documents.

#### Future

Extendable to GitHub releases. Each release becomes an entry, the water receives it and keeps it.

Create a release on GitHub and write the notes. Aino will surface them.

### Writing a Release

Follow the three moments of Aino's journey when writing release notes:

**Metsässä**, in the forest. What was being built, what changed, what was added. The decisions made before the water.

**Rannalla**, at the shore. What was released. Breaking changes, migrations, things to know before you wade in.

**Vedessä**, in the water. What is now part of the record. Fixed, shipped, gone into the deep.

```md
## Metsässä

- Added Ikiturso database layer
- Vipunen now supports locale fallback

## Rannalla

- `useDocs` API changed, `navi` is now `navigation`

## Vedessä

- Fixed surround query throwing on missing pages
- Improved transition handling in Luoto layout
```
