In the Finnish archipelago, a **luoto** is a small rocky skerry, barely above the
waterline, shaped by ice and time. A fixed point while the sea moves around it.

These guides are luodot. The content is the water.

A living collection of guides through the water world of Virtauma, from the first spring emergence to the open sea.

::tip
The examples are not yet fully functional
::
