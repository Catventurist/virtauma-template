Not all decisions are the same.

Some are a drop of dew, gone by morning.
Some are a hidden reef, requiring full attention before you proceed.
Most are somewhere between, a current finding its way.

This is a place for all of them.

## Decision types

**Kaste**, a quick yes or no. Cast it in, let it settle by morning.

**Haara**, a fork in the river. The community chooses which branch to take.

**Vuoksi**, a tidal current. Try it for a fixed period, then review whether it served.

**Pato**, consent-based. The flow stops until every stone is removed or another way is found.

**Tulva**, urgent. A flood that cannot wait. Short window, everyone responds now or the water decides.

**Karikko**, foundational. A hidden reef. Slow, deliberate, near-unanimous. Changes what everything rests on.

**Pyörre**, recurring. Returns on a rhythm until something shifts or it is consciously let go.
