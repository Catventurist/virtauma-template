Not everything that disappears leaves a body.

Some things just stop being held in anyone's memory,
and that is its own kind of death.
This is where they end up.

You can leave something here too,
if you know what you have lost.

---

location: nowhere in particular

---

They made coffee in the morning and it meant something to someone
for a while. Nobody wrote this down when it mattered.
